About me

I am an architect and I come from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. I'm a creative, dynamic person, but also eficient and organized. I love to travel and learn about different cultures. I'm interested in design and art I have a broad professional experience as architect and project manager.

I've lived in Belgium for almost four years. During this time I have acquired different academic and personal skills. Four years ago I took a Dutch course and right afterwards I began my master in heritage studies at the University of Antwerp. Meanwhile I've started working at the office DRIESEN-MEERSMAN-THOMAES Architecten (DMT). It was not always easy to combine my master studies with my work, but both experiences mutually complement each other. With the synergy between theory and practice I could learn and experiment at the same time. Now I finished my studies and I work full-time as an architect at DMT. I would like to share my work with others as well as get to know other architects' projects.
